About TIVORA Gemstones

At Tivora Gems, we specialize in bringing the timeless beauty of Ceylon sapphires and rare gemstones to the international market. Based in Sri Lanka – the island renowned as the "Gem Paradise" – we take pride in being a Registered Gem Dealer and Exporter with the Sri Lanka Gem & Jewelry Authority, ensuring that every stone we provide meets the highest standards of authenticity and quality.

Our Commitment

We believe that gemstones are more than just treasures of the earth – they are symbols of luxury, trust, and timeless elegance. Each gem in our collection is ethically sourced, carefully selected, and certified, giving our clients complete confidence in their value and origin.

Worldwide Delivery

With secure and reliable logistics, we offer global shipping, making it possible for jewelers, collectors, and investors around the world to experience the brilliance of Ceylon gems directly from the source.

Why Choose TIVORA Gems?

  • Registered and certified exporter
  • Authentic and ethically sourced gemstones
  • Direct from Sri Lanka's gem mines to the world
  • Trusted by jewelers and collectors internationally
  • Personalized service and transparency

At Tivora Gems, we are passionate about connecting gem lovers worldwide with the finest stones that carry both beauty and lasting value. Whether you are a jeweler, investor, or enthusiast, our mission is to deliver gemstones that embody trust, rarity, and timeless luxury.
